# AI_SYSTEM.md — AI System Profile (EU AI Act)

> **Template** (BOO-101). To be filled in by the operator when the solution contains an AI
> component that processes (customer) data. Curated per Reg. (EU) 2024/1689 — **NOT legal advice**;
> the operator/data protection officer decides. The dpo AUDIT mode checks the
> existence/completeness of the sections (`dpo/controls/eu-ai-act.yml`); the judgment items
> appear as REVIEW-NEEDED. Cross-reference data protection: `PRIVACY.md`.

## Purpose & Context of Use
- What does the AI system do? Who are the users/affected persons? Which decisions does it support or make (degree of automation)?

## Risk Class
- Classification: **prohibited | high-risk | limited risk | minimal risk**
- Rationale (reference Art. 5 or Art. 6 + Annex III):

## Data Processed
- Which data is processed (in particular personal/customer data)? Cross-reference `PRIVACY.md` / records of processing.

## Transparency
- How is it disclosed to users that they are interacting with an AI system or that content is AI-generated? (Art. 13/50)

## Human Oversight
- How can a human intervene, review, override or shut down? Who is responsible? (Art. 14)

## Logging
- Which events/decisions are logged? Retention period? Traceability for audits? (Art. 12)

## GPAI Use
- Is a general-purpose AI / foundation model used or provided? Which one? What obligations arise? (Chap. V)

## Open Items (REVIEW-NEEDED)
- Is a conformity assessment required? A fundamental rights impact assessment? Which operator/legal decisions are open?
